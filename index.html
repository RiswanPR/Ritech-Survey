About

Established in 2010, Ritech Surveying & Engineering has grown into one of Kerala’s most trusted engineering survey companies. With over 15 years of experience, we bring precision, professionalism, and innovation to every project.

Our expert team of surveyors and engineers uses the latest technology to deliver accurate, cost-effective results on time—every time.

Services

We offer expert land and aerial surveying solutions using the latest technology to deliver fast, accurate, and cost-effective results.

Land Surveying & Mapping

Detailed land surveys for all property types using Total Station, DGPS, and RTK drone technology.

Road Survey & Engineering

Complete road project services, including feasibility studies, DPR preparation, and alignment design.

Aerial Survey & Mapping

High-resolution aerial mapping using UAVs for faster, more detailed data over large or complex areas.

Land Development Solutions

Infrastructure planning for residential layouts, villas, and resorts—including roads, drainage, pipelines, and landscaping.

GIS Mapping & Data Services

Digital mapping and GIS database development for effective asset visualization and management.
